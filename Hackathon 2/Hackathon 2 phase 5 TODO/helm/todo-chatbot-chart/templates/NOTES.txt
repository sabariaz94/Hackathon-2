== Getting Started ==

1. Check pod status:
   kubectl get pods -n {{ .Values.namespace }}

2. Access the frontend:
   minikube service frontend-service -n {{ .Values.namespace }}

3. Check backend health:
   kubectl exec -it deployment/{{ include "todo-chatbot.fullname" . }}-backend -n {{ .Values.namespace }} -- curl localhost:{{ .Values.service.backend.port }}/health
